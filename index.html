FAQ

Can I send emails and convert them to Twitter messages?
Yes. Just define an email and a Twitter endpoint.
Do you guarantee delivery?
Yes. Your data is stored until delivery is done. If the receiving endpoint is not reachable and you have reached the maximum storage limit, the pipe will stop accepting new messages. In this case, either purhace more storage or wake up the receiving endpoint.
